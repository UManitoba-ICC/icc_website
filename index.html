About ICC

International Students' Community Center is made by international students, for international students. We hope to make the university of Manitoba feel welcoming to all the different communities and foster a hub of connection and passion. We aim to provide support to international students, as well as hold events to help build connections within and beyond the international community. We are here for you and each other. Feel free to contact us at any time!

What are student communities?

There are many ways for you to make connections at UM. Whether you are looking for other students who share your interests, are in the same academic program, or are committed to advancing similar causes, this is the place for you to connect!

The following are recognized communities at UM.
  • 2SLGBTQIA+
  • Accessibility
  • Black
  • Indigenous
  • Interfaith Spiritual
  • International
  • Mature Students
  • Student Parent
  • Women
